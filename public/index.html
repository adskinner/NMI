Pt 1. Finding Your Call to Action

What is a 'Call To Action'? (CTA)


What action do you want your visitors to your website to make?

CTA Examples


  • user fills out contact form
  • user signs up for your newsletter
  • user buys your product online

What type of platform is the best for what type of CTA?


  • Wordpress
  • Shopify
  • Squarespace/Wix/Weebly

Wordpress


  • Most used CMS platform
  • Good for basic + complex websites
  • Many Wordpress developers/experts available
  • Open source! i.e. free - affordable!

Shopify


  • Platform tailored for ecommerce
  • You can get a store up and running quickly
  • Paid platform (aka 24/7 support available)
  • Can manage large online stores with high-level of customization

Squarespace/Wix/Weebly


  • Drag and drop friendly template builder
  • Paid platform (aka 24/7 support available)
  • Limited customization available not super friendly for developers

Overview: What type of platform is best for your CTA


  • I want someone to fill out a contact form: Wordpress or Squarespace/Wix/Weebly
  • I want someone to buy my product: Shopify or Wordpress
  • I want a website that allows me to grow & customize. Maybe hire an expert down the line: Wordpress or Shopify
